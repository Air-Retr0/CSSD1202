FAQ: Carbon Footprint

What is a carbon footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (mainly carbon dioxide and methane) that are produced by our actions!

How can I calculate my personal carbon footprint?

We have our very own Terra-Point calculator for you to use here.

What are the main contributors to an individual's carbon footprint?

There are many contributors, but the main and biggest include electricity usage, heat, and transportation.

How does air travel impact my carbon footprint?

Air travel significantly impacts one's carbon footprint due to its extremely high emissions per passenger. For example, one flight from London to San Francisco emits more than twice the emissions produced by a family car in over a year!

What are some easy ways to reduce my carbon footprint at home?

There are numerous small ways that can reduce your carbon footprint by a considerable amount over time, and can be found in our tip page!

How does a plant-based diet affect my carbon footprint?

Having a plant-based diet actually reduces one's carbon footprint by a significant amount. This is due to many factors such as greenhouse gas emissions, land and water uses, and energy efficiency!

What is the impact of fast fashion on the environment?

Fast fashion yields a greatly negative impact on the environment, as statistically proven by Earth.org, in which it contributes heavily in terms of carbon emissions, water consumption, and waste production.

How can businesses reduce their carbon footprint?

Businesses can reduce their carbon footprint in numerous ways, involving carbon reduction targets and waste, conserving energy and water, and overall educating employees to raise awareness.
