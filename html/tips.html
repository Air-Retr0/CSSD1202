How to Reduce Your Carboon Footprint

At Home

  • Turn off lights when leaving a room
  • Unplug electronics when not in use
  • Use LED light bulbs
  • Lower thermostat in winter by 1-2 degrees
  • Use cold water for laundry when possible
  • Air dry clothes instead of using dryer
  • Take shorter showers

In the Kitchen

  • Use reusable shopping bags
  • Buy local and seasonal produce
  • Reduce meat consumption
  • Use a microwave or toaster oven for small meals
  • Compost food scraps
  • Use a water filter instead of bottled water

Transportation

  • Walk or bike for short trips
  • Use public transportation
  • Carpool when possible
  • Combine errands to reduce driving
  • Maintain proper tire pressure

Shopping and Waste

  • Buy second-hand items
  • Repair items instead of replacing
  • Recycle properly
  • Use reusable water bottles
  • Avoid single-use plastics

Every small action counts in reducing your carbon footprint!
